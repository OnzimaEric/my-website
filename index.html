Our Mission & Vision

At Protevia International, we believe that everyone deserves to work and live in safe, healthy environments. Our mission is to eliminate preventable workplace injuries, death and environmental hazards through education, advocacy, and action.

In line with vision zero philosophy, we envision a world where safety transcends borders, where communities are empowered with knowledge, and where sustainable practices protect both people and planet for generations to come.
